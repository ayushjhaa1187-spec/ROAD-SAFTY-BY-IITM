# Stage 1 Presentation Outline (7 Slides)

### Slide 1: Title & Vision
- Project Name: **RoadWatch**
- Tagline: AI-Powered Geospatial Governance for Safer Roads
- Team Details

### Slide 2: The Problem
- High road fatality rates due to poor infrastructure.
- Delayed reporting & lack of transparency.
- Disconnect between citizen observations and authority actions.

### Slide 3: The Solution
- A modular platform for citizen reporting and administrative monitoring.
- Real-time geospatial tracking (PostGIS).
- AI summaries for administrative efficiency.

### Slide 4: Key Features (MVP)
- **Report Hazards**: Simple 3-click reporting for citizens.
- **Authority Dashboard**: Live map view and priority queue for officials.
- **Status Lifecycle**: Transparent tracking from "Open" to "Resolved".

### Slide 5: Tech Stack & Architecture
- Next.js + Supabase + PostGIS.
- Modular core design (ready for DriveLegal or RoadSoS pivot).
- High-fidelity premium UI.

### Slide 6: AI Integration
- LLM-powered complaint generation.
- Automated severity labeling.
- Authority suggestion based on geography and issue type.

### Slide 7: Roadmap & Scalability
- **Week 3-4**: Deep analytics and heatmap reporting.
- **Week 5-6**: Integration with traffic police (DriveLegal) or Emergency Services (RoadSoS).
- **Scale**: Multi-city deployment via standardized schemas.
