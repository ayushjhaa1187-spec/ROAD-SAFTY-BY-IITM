### Slide 2: The Problem
- Fragmented reporting (WhatsApp/Twitter/Calls).
- No centralized "Risk Map" for authorities.
- Lack of transparency in the resolution lifecycle.
- Infrastructure defects (potholes/cracks) are the #1 invisible killers.

### Slide 3: The Solution
- **Unified Workflow**: Citizen → AI Verification → Authority Action.
- **Multimodal Analysis**: Integrating text, photos (YOLOv8), and sensor data (Vibrations).
- **Public Transparency**: Live hotspot map and resolution timeline.

### Slide 4: AI Architecture
- **Vision**: YOLOv8 microservice for automated defect detection.
- **Reasoning**: Gemini-powered complaint summarization and risk scoring.
- **Social**: RAG-based DriveLegal for traffic law transparency.
- **Fusion**: Telemetry roughness computation.

### Slide 5: The Demo (5-Step Pitch)
1. **Report**: Citizen takes a photo of a pothole.
2. **Detect**: YOLOv8 auto-tags the defect and confidence level.
3. **Analyze**: Gemini generates a formal PWD complaint.
4. **Intelligence**: The data updates the city-wide Hotspot Map.
5. **Resolve**: Admin verifies and moves the report to "In Progress."

### Slide 6: Alignment & Impact
- Aligns with CoERS "AI for Road Safety" vision.
- Data-driven maintenance prioritization (saves taxpayer money).
- Reduces resolution time from weeks to days.
- Scalable beyond Chennai to any smart city ecosystem.
